About Us

Position Development is a worker-run software development company, with a focus on independent media, cultural institutions and socially minded organizations.

Our core business idea is that by organizing a team specialized in the needs of independent/radical media, and by being worker run -- without owners skimming off the top -- we can serve the software needs of these organizations that we care about better and cheaper than anyone else. We already have some great clients and it seems to be working.

Our Clients

Verso Books

Verso Books is the largest independent, radical publishing house in the English-speaking world, publishing one hundred books a year.

Verso has a full online bookstore selling print and electronic versions of their catalog of thousands of titles. We maintain this ecommerce system, and its backend integration with warehouse fulfillment, as well as doing all new feature development.

The Criterion Collection

The Criterion Collection is dedicated to gathering the greatest films from around the world and publishing them in editions that offer the highest technical quality and award-winning, original supplements.

We maintain and improve all aspects of Criterion's online presence, from their online store where they sell collection films, to the writing and commentary on films, database of filmmakers and films, and various other parts of the online experience. We took the project on as a large existing seven year old codebase, and have been making improvements while delivering new features.

The New Inquiry

The New Inquiry is a space for discussion that aspires to enrich cultural and public life by putting all available resources—both digital and material—toward the promotion and exploration of ideas.

We built a new digital subscription system for them, that manages recurring payments and synchronizes subscribers into mailing lists that they use to send out issues.

Jacobin Magazine

Jacobin Magazine is a leading voice of the American left, offering socialist perspectives on politics, economics, and culture.

We built a print and digital subscription and paywall system, integrated into their existing Wordpress site. In addition to standard list management features like address updates and issue releases, it has fine grained mailing list control, donations, and extras like lifetime subscriptions.

The Real Food Challenge

The Real Food Challenge leverages the power of youth and universities to create a healthy, fair and green food system.

We built the Calculator, an interactive tool for data entry and analysis. Students upload food purchasing data from their universities, rate items according to specific criteria, and produce reports based on the results. The Calculator is part of an ongoing campaign by The Real Food Challenge to shift more food purchasing local, ecologically responsible, fair trade, and humane sources.
