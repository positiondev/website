Our Mission

Position Development is a worker-run software development company, with a focus on independent media, cultural institutions and socially minded organizations.

Our core business idea is that by organizing a team specialized in the needs of independent/radical media, and by being worker run, we can serve the software needs of these organizations that we care about better and cheaper than anyone else. We already have some great clients and it seems to be working.
