We're looking for people to join us.

Position Development is a worker-run software development company, with the possibility of expanding as a full-service firm, with software development at our core. We are selective with clients and place strong emphasis on independent media, cultural institutions, socially minded organizations, and opportunities to grow our collective skill sets for unique and creative projects.

The way we work is based on the particular needs of our workers. There is no management layer dictating from on-high. Position Development is worker-run, which means we collectively decide how we want to work.

Role:
Partner Track

We're looking for an experienced developer to join our team. Ideally you've built and maintained customer-facing production web applications. It's a plus if your past work has dealt with money, background data processing, or anything else beyond simple CRUD. We use automated testing extensively and would expect you to as well. Some of our work involves maintaining and improving large legacy code bases and we hope you'd be excited to take that on. Our team works on many projects and part of this role will mean that at times you will be responsible for taking the lead on a given project. That could include working with clients to figure out requirements, implementing features, testing, and delivering the result.

Compensation: $60-80/hr based on experience, eventually transitioning to ownership and cooperative profit sharing.
