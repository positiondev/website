Real Food Challenge Calculator

The Calculator is a interactive tool for data entry and analysis created for the non-profit Real Food Challenge. Students upload food purchasing data from their universities, rate items according to specific criteria, and produce reports based on the results. The Calculator is part of an ongoing campaign by Real Food Challenge to shift more food purchasing local, ecologically responsible, fair trade, and humane sources.

Jacobin Magazine Subscription and Fullfillment

We built a full subscription and fullfillment system for Jacobin Magazine, a socialist quarterly magazine based in New York. The system allowed paywall access to online content, address and renewal management for subscribers, a mailing list, as well as print ready export lists for their distributor. The entire system was integrated seamlessly with their existing Wordpress frontend.
