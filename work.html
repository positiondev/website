Who we're looking for

We're looking for a web developer who is excited by what we do to join our team onsite at our office in Brooklyn.

We work collaboratively - pair programming regularly - and mentorship of apprentice developers is a key part of our work. We work through various stages of backend development - from meeting with clients to figure out requirements on new projects to working on existing "legacy" codebases.

Currently, we have two main products/projects, both of which evolved out of custom software. One is an online independent bookstore platform that was originally developed for Verso Books and is now being adapted for other independent publishers. It's a Rails application running on AWS.

The other product is a magazine platform that was originally developed for Jacobin magazine but is in the process of being adapted for other publications. It's made up of a Wordpress site for content authorship and a Haskell backend for subscription/fulfillment.

We hope you'd be excited to work on these and other projects. Both Jacobin and Verso have said these platforms are a big part of their recent success, and this type of development and adaptation is the main focus of Position Development.
